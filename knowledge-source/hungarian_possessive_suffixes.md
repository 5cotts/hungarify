# Hungarian Possessive Suffixes by Vowel Harmony

## Vowel Types
- **High (Front) vowels**: i, í, é, ö, ő, ü, ű
- **Low (Back) vowels**: a, á, o, ó, u, ú
- **Mixed (Neutral) vowels**: e (can go with either)

## Possessive Suffixes Table

| Person | High Vowel Words | Low Vowel Words | Mixed Vowel Words |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **1st singular (my)** | -em/-öm/-üm | -am/-om/-um | -em |
| **2nd singular (your)** | -ed/-öd/-üd | -ad/-od/-ud | -ed |
| **3rd singular (his/her/its)** | -e/-je | -a/-ja | -e/-je |
| **1st plural (our)** | -ünk | -unk | -ünk |
| **2nd plural (your)** | -etek/-ötök | -atok/-otok | -etek |
| **3rd plural (their)** | -ük/-jük | -uk/-juk | -ük/-jük |

**Note:** These suffixes are used after both consonants and vowels. The linking vowel (a/e/o/ö/u/ü) is determined by the vowel harmony of the word.

## Detailed Rules

### 1st Person Singular (-m)
- **High vowel words**: -em, -öm, or -üm
  - szék → szék**em** (my chair) - ends in consonant, high vowel word
  - kert → kert**em** (my garden) - ends in consonant, mixed vowel word (takes high endings)
  - szőke → szők**ém** (my blonde) - ends in vowel "e", high vowel word
- **Low vowel words**: -am, -om, or -um
  - ház → ház**am** (my house) - ends in consonant, low vowel word
  - autó → aut**óm** (my car) - ends in vowel "ó", low vowel word
  - macska → macsk**ám** (my cat) - ends in vowel "a", low vowel word

### 2nd Person Singular (-d)
- **High vowel words**: -ed, -öd, or -üd
  - szék → szék**ed** (your chair) - ends in consonant, high vowel word
  - kert → kert**ed** (your garden) - ends in consonant, mixed vowel word
  - szőke → szők**éd** (your blonde) - ends in vowel "e", high vowel word
- **Low vowel words**: -ad, -od, or -ud
  - ház → ház**ad** (your house) - ends in consonant, low vowel word
  - autó → aut**ód** (your car) - ends in vowel "ó", low vowel word
  - macska → macsk**ád** (your cat) - ends in vowel "a", low vowel word

### 3rd Person Singular (-a/-e/-ja/-je)
- **Low vowel words**: -a (after consonant) or -ja (after vowel)
  - ház → ház**a** (his/her house) - ends in consonant
  - autó → autó**ja** (his/her car) - ends in vowel "ó"
  - macska → macsk**ája** (his/her cat) - ends in vowel "a"
- **High vowel words**: -e (after consonant) or -je (after vowel)
  - kert → kert**e** (his/her garden) - ends in consonant, mixed vowel word (takes high endings)
  - szék → szék**e** (his/her chair) - ends in consonant, high vowel word
  - szőke → szők**éje** (his/her blonde) - ends in vowel "e", high vowel word

## Examples

| Noun | Type | My | Your | His/Her | Our | Your (pl) | Their |
|------|------|----|----|---------|-----|-----------|-------|
| ház (house) | Low | ház**am** | ház**ad** | ház**a** | ház**unk** | ház**atok** | ház**uk** |
| autó (car) | Low | aut**óm** | aut**ód** | autó**ja** | aut**ónk** | aut**ótok** | autó**juk** |
| kert (garden) | Mixed | kert**em** | kert**ed** | kert**e** | kert**ünk** | kert**etek** | kert**ük** |
| macska (cat) | Low | macsk**ám** | macsk**ád** | macsk**ája** | macsk**ánk** | macsk**átok** | macsk**ájuk** |
| szék (chair) | High | szék**em** | szék**ed** | szék**e** | szék**ünk** | szék**etek** | szék**ük** |
| anya (mother) | Low | any**ám** | any**ád** | any**a** | any**ánk** | any**átok** | any**uk** |

## Key Points
1. **Linking vowels are always needed**: Whether the word ends in a consonant or vowel, you add a linking vowel (a/e/o/ö/u/ü) before the possessive marker (-m, -d, etc.)
2. **Vowel harmony determines the linking vowel**: 
   - Low vowel words → use -a/-o/-u linking vowels
   - High vowel words → use -e/-ö/-ü linking vowels
   - Mixed vowel words (e) → usually take high vowel endings
3. **3rd person**: Uses -a/-ja for low vowels, -e/-je for high vowels. The -j- appears when the word ends in a vowel.
4. **Plural forms**: Follow vowel harmony rules strictly (-unk/-ünk, -atok/-etek/-otok/-ötök, -uk/-ük/-juk/-jük)
5. **Mixed vowels (e)**: Usually take high vowel endings (-em, -ed, -e, -ünk, etc.)
